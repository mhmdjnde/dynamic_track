Trapez (1)
Do one of these
1

For dumbbell trapezius exercises, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells in front of your thighs with a neutral grip. For dumbbell shrugs, hold the dumbbells by your sides. Both exercises engage the upper traps, with the rear delts being activated as stabilizers.

2

For trapezius exercises, pull the dumbbells upwards, focusing on the upper traps. For shrugs, lift the dumbbells by shrugging your shoulders upwards, squeezing the traps and rear delts at the top of the movement.

3

Lower the weights back to the starting position in a controlled manner, ensuring the rear delts are engaged as stabilizers throughout the movement.
