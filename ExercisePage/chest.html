Barbell / Smith-Machine Bench Press (1)
Novice
1

Lie flat on a bench with feet planted on the ground. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.

2

Unrack the bar and slowly lower it to your chest, keeping your elbows at a 45-degree angle.

3

Press the bar back up explosively, fully extending your arms. Keep your core engaged. Repeat for reps.



Machine Pec Fly (2)
Novice
1

Adjust seat height so handles align with chest. Sit and grip handles with elbows slightly bent.

2

Slowly bring handles together in front, keeping tension. Focus on squeezing chest muscles.

3

Return handles to starting position with controlled motion. Keep chest up throughout. Repeat for reps.





Dips / Dips(using Bench) (3)
Novice
1

Grip the parallel bars with your arms fully extended and your body straight.

2

Lower yourself by bending your elbows until your upper arms are parallel to the floor.

3

Push yourself back up to the starting position by straightening your arms. Keep your core engaged. Repeat for reps.



Normal Pushups / Feet-Elevated Pushups (4)
Novice
1

For decline push-ups, place your feet on an elevated surface and your hands on the floor, shoulder-width apart.

2

Lower your chest toward the floor while keeping your core tight and back straight. Push back up to the starting position.

3

For incline push-ups, place your hands on an elevated surface and feet on the ground. Lower your chest, then push back up. Repeat for reps.
