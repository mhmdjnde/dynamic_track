Abs / Hangin-Knee-Raises (1)
Novice
1

For crunches, lie on your back with your knees bent and hands behind your head. Engage your core.

2

Lift your upper back off the ground, squeezing your abs at the top. Lower back down with control.

3

For hanging knee raises, hang from a pull-up bar with arms extended. Lift your knees towards your chest, then lower them back down.



Lower Abs (2)
Novice
1

For leg raises, lie flat on your back with legs straight and hands under your lower back for support.

2

Lift your legs up towards the ceiling while keeping them straight. Engage your lower abs at the top.

3

For reverse crunches, bend your knees and bring them towards your chest, lifting your hips off the ground. Lower back down with control.





Russian-Twist (3)
Novice
1

Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet slightly off the ground. Lean back slightly to engage your core.

2

Hold a weight or clasp your hands together. Rotate your torso to one side, bringing your hands towards the floor.

3

Twist to the opposite side in a controlled motion. Keep your core tight and repeat for reps.
