Lat Pulldown Machine (1)
Novice
1

Sit down at the lat pulldown machine with your feet flat on the floor and knees secured under the pad. Grab the bar with a wide grip, ensuring your palms are facing forward. This exercise primarily targets the lats, with the rear delts activated as stabilizers throughout the movement.

2

3

Slowly release the bar back to the starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement and keeping your rear delts engaged as stabilizers.



Cable Rows (2)
Novice
1

Sit at the cable row machine with your feet secured and knees slightly bent. Grab the handle with both hands, palms facing each other, and keep your back straight. This exercise targets the lats, with the rear delts engaged as stabilizers throughout the movement.

2

Pull the handle towards your torso, focusing on squeezing your shoulder blades together and engaging your rear delts as you row the weight. Keep your elbows close to your body and avoid shrugging your shoulders.

3

Slowly return the handle to the starting position, maintaining control and ensuring the rear delts remain activated as stabilizers during the motion.





T-Bar-Row (3)
Novice
1

For T-Bar Rows, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, bending slightly at the knees and hips, and grab the handles with both hands. For Bent-Over Rows, bend at the hips and knees to lower your torso while keeping your back straight, and hold a barbell with an overhand grip. Both exercises target the lats, with the rear delts activated as stabilizers throughout the movement.

2

For T-Bar Rows, pull the handles towards your chest, focusing on engaging the lats and rear delts as you row the weight. For Bent-Over Rows, pull the barbell towards your lower ribcage, keeping your elbows close to your body and your rear delts engaged. Keep your core tight to protect your lower back.

3

Slowly return the weight to the starting position, ensuring control and maintaining rear delt activation as stabilizers. Focus on the lats throughout the movement and avoid shrugging your shoulders.
